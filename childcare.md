Parents looking for childcare during the Colloquia can contact [Turtles Nursery](https://www.turtlesnursery.com/contact) or [MyKiddy Babysitters](https://mykiddysitter.co.uk/contact/).

[//]: # Turtles Nursery is located eight minutes on foot from LSE and can take children aged three or four.

[//]: # MyKiddy Babysitters are a professional babysitting agency whose services include hotel sitting.
