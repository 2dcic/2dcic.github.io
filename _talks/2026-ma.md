title: An Exponential Improvement for Ramsey Lower Bounds (and Beyond)
excerpt_separator: <!--more-->

---

We prove a new lower bound on the Ramsey number $r(\ell, C\ell)$ for any constant $C> 1$ and sufficiently large $\ell$, showing that there exists $\varepsilon(C) > 0$ such that $r(\ell, C\ell) \ge \left(p_C^{-1/2} + \varepsilon(C)\right)^\ell$, where $p_C$ denotes the unique solution in $(0, 1/2)$ satisfying $C = \log p_C / \log (1 - p_C)$. This provides the first exponential improvement over the classical lower bound by Erd\H{o}s since 1947. We will also aim to discuss some recent development related to this approach. 
Joint work with Wujie Shen and Shengjie Xie.
